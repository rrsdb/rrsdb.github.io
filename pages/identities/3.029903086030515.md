## Combinatorial interpretation of product

- $r(n)$ is the number of [partitions](../partitions.html#integer_partitions) of $n$ into parts not congruent to $0, \pm 3\pmod{27}$.

## [Asymptotic formula](../asymptotics.html)

$ \displaystyle r(n) \sim\frac{e^{\frac{4 \pi  \sqrt{n}}{3 \sqrt{3}}} \csc \left(\frac{\pi }{27}\right) \csc \left(\frac{2 \pi }{27}\right) \csc \left(\frac{4 \pi }{27}\right) \csc \left(\frac{5 \pi }{27}\right) \csc \left(\frac{2 \pi }{9}\right) \sec \left(\frac{\pi }{54}\right) \sec \left(\frac{\pi }{18}\right) \sec \left(\frac{5 \pi }{54}\right) \sec \left(\frac{7 \pi }{54}\right) \sec \left(\frac{11 \pi }{54}\right) \sec \left(\frac{13 \pi }{54}\right)}{6144 \sqrt{2} \sqrt[4]{3} n^{3/4}}$ as $n\to\infty. $
