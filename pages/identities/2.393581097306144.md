## Combinatorial interpretation of product

- $r(n)$ is the number of [partitions](../partitions.html#integer_partitions) of $n$ into parts that are odd or congruent to $8\pmod{16}$.
- $r(n)$ is the number of [partitions](../partitions.html#integer_partitions) of $n$ where odd parts must not be repeated and even parts must not be congruent to $0, \pm 2\pmod{16}$.

## [Asymptotic formula](../asymptotics.html)

$ \displaystyle r(n) \sim\frac{\sqrt[4]{\frac{3}{2}} e^{\frac{1}{2} \sqrt{\frac{3}{2}} \pi  \sqrt{n}}}{8 n^{3/4}}$ as $n\to\infty. $
