# Article for infinite product with [signature](../product_signature.html) 8:(0,0,-1,2,-1,0,0,0) 


$$ F(q) := \sum_{n=0}^\infty r(n)q^n := \frac{f(-q^3,-q^5)}{\varphi(-q^4)} = \frac{(q^{3},q^{5}; q^{8})_\infty}{(q^{4}; q^{8})_\infty^{2}} $$

## Combinatorial interpretation of product

- $(-1)^n r(n)$ is the number of [overpartitions](../partitions.html#overpartitions) of $n$ where overlined parts are congruent to $0, 3, 4, 5 \pmod{8}$ and nonoverlined parts are congruent to $4\pmod{8}$.

  
## [Asymptotic formula](../asymptotics.html)

$ \displaystyle (-1)^n r(n) \sim \frac{e^{\frac{\pi  \sqrt{n}}{2}}}{8 n^{3/4}} $ as $ n\to\infty. $
