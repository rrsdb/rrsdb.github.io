## Combinatorial interpretation of product

- $r(n)$ is the number of [partitions](../partitions.html#integer_partitions) of $n$ into parts not congruent to $0, 16, \pm 4, \pm 6, \pm 10\pmod{32}$.
- $r(n)$ is the number of [overpartitions](../partitions.html#overpartitions) of $n$ where nonoverlined parts are even but not multiples of $16$, and overlined parts are odd or congruent to $\pm 2\pmod{16}$.

## [Asymptotic formula](../asymptotics.html)

$ \displaystyle r(n) \sim\frac{e^{\frac{\pi  \sqrt{n}}{\sqrt{2}}}}{8 \sqrt[4]{2} n^{3/4}}$ as $n\to\infty. $
