## Combinatorial interpretation of product

- $r(n)$ is the number of [overpartitions](../partitions.html#overpartitions) of $n$ into parts where nonoverlined parts are congruent to $\pm 1, \pm 4, \pm 6\pmod{14}$.

## [Asymptotic formula](../asymptotics.html)

$ \displaystyle r(n) \sim\frac{\sqrt[4]{\frac{13}{21}} e^{\sqrt{\frac{13}{21}} \pi  \sqrt{n}} \sin \left(\frac{\pi }{14}\right) \csc ^2\left(\frac{\pi }{7}\right)}{8 n^{3/4}}$ as $n\to\infty. $
