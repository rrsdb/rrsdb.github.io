# Article for infinite product with [signature](../product_signature.html) 8:(-1,0,0,2,0,0,-1,0)


$$ F(q) := \sum_{n=0}^\infty r(n)q^n := \frac{f(-q,-q^7)}{\phi(-q^4)} = \frac{(q^{},q^{7}; q^{8})_\infty}{(q^{4}; q^{8})_\infty^{2}} $$

## Combinatorial interpretation of product

- $(-1)^n r(n)$ is the number of [overpartitions](../partitions.html#overpartitions) of $n$ where overlined parts are congruent to $0, 4, \pm 1\pmod{8}$ and nonoverlined parts are $4\pmod{8}$.

## [Asymptotic formula](../asymptotics.html)

$ \displaystyle (-1)^n r(n) \sim \frac{e^{\frac{\pi  \sqrt{n}}{2}}}{8 n^{3/4}} $ as $ n\to\infty. $
