# Article for infinite product with [signature](../product_signature.html) 10:(2,1,2,1,0,1,2,1,2,0) 


$$ F(q) := \sum_{n=0}^\infty r(n)q^n := \frac{f(-q^5,-q^5)}{\varphi(-q)} = \frac{1}{(q^{},q^{3},q^{7},q^{9}; q^{10})_\infty^{2}(q^{2},q^{4},q^{6},q^{8}; q^{10})_\infty} $$

## Combinatorial interpretation of product

- $r(n)$ is the number of [two-color partitions](../partitions.html#colored_partitions) of $n$ where no part is a multiple of $5$, even parts may only appear in blue, and odd parts may appear in either red or blue.

## [Asymptotic formula](../asymptotics.html)

$ \displaystyle r(n) \sim \frac{e^{\frac{2 \pi  \sqrt{n}}{\sqrt{5}}}}{2\ 5^{3/4} n^{3/4}} $ as $ n\to\infty. $
