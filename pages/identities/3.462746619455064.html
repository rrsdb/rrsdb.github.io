Article for infinite product with signature 1:(1)

\[ F(q) := \sum_{n=0}^\infty r(n)q^n := \frac{1}{f(-q)} = \frac{1}{(q;q)_\infty}\]

Combinatorial interpretation of product

\(r(n)\) is the number of partitions of \(n\).

Asymptotic formula for \(r(n)\)

\(\displaystyle r(n) \sim \frac{e^{\sqrt{\frac{2}{3}} \pi \sqrt{n}}}{4 \sqrt{3} n} \) as \( n\to\infty. \) Due to Hardy and Ramanujan (1918, p. 79, Eq. (1.41)).
