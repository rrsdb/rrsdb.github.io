## Combinatorial interpretation of product

- $r(n)$ is the number of [partitions](../partitions.html#integer_partitions) of $n$ into parts congruent to $\pm 1, \pm 4, \pm 5, \pm 7, \pm 8, \pm 9, \pm 11, \pm 13, \pm 16, \pm 16, \pm 17 \pmod{36}$

## [Asymptotic formula](../asymptotics.html)

$ \displaystyle r(n) \sim\frac{\left(\sqrt{3}-1\right) e^{\frac{2 \pi  \sqrt{n}}{3}} \sin \left(\frac{\pi }{18}\right) \csc ^3\left(\frac{\pi }{9}\right) \sec \left(\frac{\pi }{9}\right)}{96 n^{3/4}}$ as $n\to\infty. $
