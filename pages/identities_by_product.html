Infinite products associated with Rogers–Ramanujan type identities

This page is a list of infinite products associated with Rogers–Ramanujan type identities. Each listed product links to its associated home page, and can be represented by one or more series representations. The list is sorted by the modulus of a product as determined by the product signature.

Use the search bar on the left to jump to a particular modulus. Click on any product to see the series representations.

For help with notation, see the definition of the \(q\)-Pochhammer symbol and of Ramanujan's theta functions.
