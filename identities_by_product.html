Rogers–Ramanujan type identities

Identities by their product representations

This page is a catalog of known Rogers–Ramanujan type identities, sorted by their product representations. Each product may be associated to several \(q\)-series. The modulus of a product is determined by the product signature.

Use the search bar on the left to jump to a particular modulus. Click on any product to see the series representations.

For help with notation, see the definition of the \(q\)-Pochhammer symbol and of Ramanujan's theta functions.
