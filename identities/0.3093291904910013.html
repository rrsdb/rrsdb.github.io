Article for infinite product with signature 4:(-1,-1,-1,0)

\[ F(q) := \sum_{n=0}^\infty r(n)q^n := \frac{f(-q,-q)}{\psi(-q)} = (q,q^2,q^3; q^4)_\infty\]

Combinatorial interpretation of product

Let \(e(n)\) and \(o(n)\) respectively count the number of partitions of \(n\) with an even and odd number of distinct parts which may not be divisible by 4. Then \(r(n) = e(n) - o(n)\).
